A Pokémon adventure based in the Torren region!

Pokémon are creatures of all shapes and sizes who live in the wild or alongside humans. Pokémon are raised and commanded by their owners (called “Trainers”).
During their adventures, Pokémon grow and become more experienced and even, on occasion, evolve into stronger Pokémon. There are currently more than 700 creatures that inhabit the Pokémon universe.
